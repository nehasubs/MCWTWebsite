The World's Initial Reaction


From the results of my video it is obvious that living without technology would shock at lot of people. Many people would not only be angry that they can’t use their beloved phones or computers.

However, some people would be wondering how they would interact with their loved ones. I personally would wonder how I would FaceTime my sister who lives in California.

Although this is a challenge, that will be met with a lot anger, society’s initial reaction will change throughout the next 7 days.
